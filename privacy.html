Privacy

Kulay is a colour-on-a-map app. It stores the pins you make and the rough location they were made at. That’s the shape of what we collect.

What we collect

  • The colours you pin and where you pinned them.
  • Anonymous device identifiers used to keep your pins attached to your device.

What we don’t collect

  • Your name, email, or social identity.
  • Your precise location history outside of active pins.
  • Contacts, photos, or microphone input.

How we use it

Pins are shown on the shared map so other Kulay users can see the colours around them. That’s the whole product. We don’t sell your data; there are no ads.

Questions

This is a stub policy. A full, lawyer-reviewed version will land before public launch. Reach out at hello@kulay.co in the meantime.
