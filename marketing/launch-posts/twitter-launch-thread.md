## Thread (post immediately after pinned tweet, as replies)

### 1/6 — Walk

> 1/ The whole game has four verbs. The first is **walk**.
>
> Open the app. Start a session. Put your phone away. Just walk.
> Your phone tracks your path on a map.
>
> That's the input. There isn't a controller.
>
> [Still: a phone lock-screen showing a discreet "Kulay session
> active — 12 min" notification.]

### 2/6 — Splat

> 2/ The second verb: **splat**.
>
> As you walk, your path draws on the map in your colour. A thick
> translucent stroke, like wet ink on paper. You don't have to look
> at the screen for it to happen. It just keeps drawing.
>
> [GIF: trail drawing itself along a winding path on a neutral
> grey map, in a coral colour.]

### 3/6 — Circuit

> 3/ The third verb: **circuit**.
>
> Close the loop. Walk back to (or near) where your trail started.
> Doesn't have to be a perfect circle — any closed shape works.
>
> A bad route is just a more interesting shape.
>
> [Still: a closed-loop path on the map, with a small "loop
> closed" pulse animation at the join point.]

### 4/6 — Flood

> 4/ The fourth verb: **flood**.
>
> The instant you close the loop, the area inside floods with your
> colour. A whole patch of the city, painted by a single walk.
>
> That patch is yours. Other players can see it. They can walk
> over it and reclaim it.
>
> [GIF: closed loop fills from the join-point outward with
> translucent coral. The fill takes about a second.]

### 5/6 — Why no score

> 5/ Three things this game does NOT have, on purpose:
>
> · No step counter. No daily goal. No "you've been inactive."
> · No leaderboard, no friends list, no follower count.
> · No combat, no creatures, no PvE encounters.
>
> The map is the feedback. Your colours are the score.

### 6/6 — Where to find it

> 6/ iOS only at launch. TestFlight invites going out in waves
> over the next few weeks.
>
> If a game made of walks sounds like a thing you'd try, the
> waitlist is at https://kulay.co.
>
> Replies open. Tell me what you'd want from a walking game.
