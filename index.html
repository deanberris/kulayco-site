A different kind of walking app

Walking is the goal.
Territory is the reason.

Most apps count steps and call it motivation. Kulay gives your walks consequence. Every block you walk can be yours. Every day you skip, it starts to fade. The city is always changing — you either show up or lose ground.

How it works

Walk a loop.
Flood the block.

Open the app. Start walking. Your trail draws behind you in real time. Walk any route that returns to where you started — and the entire enclosed block floods with your colour. The longer your loop, the more you claim.

You earn ink by walking. Ink is what fuels each claim. Walk every day and your balance grows. Stay home and it shrinks — along with your territory.

Your identity

One colour.
All yours.

Pick a colour when you start. Every block you claim, every trail you walk — it all wears that colour. Other players see your mark spreading across the map. You see theirs.

No avatars. No usernames on a podium. Just colour on a map that tells you who’s been where — and who’s been busy.

Stay active or lose ground

Your territory
fades when you stop.

Territory isn’t permanent. Cells lose strength over time — the map shows your weakest blocks in orange. Walk back through them and they reinforce. Walk a new loop and you expand. The city doesn’t freeze while you’re away.

This is the habit loop: walk → claim → maintain → expand. Each walk has stakes. Each route is a decision.

  • Walk daily to maintain strength
  • New loops expand your territory
  • Fading cells shown in orange — walk there to reinforce
  • Rivals can contest your weakest blocks

Better together

Build a crew.
Hold the neighbourhood.

Invite friends to join your crew. Your crew shares your colour — their walks reinforce your territory too. When you need backup, call a rally and your whole crew gets notified.

The best held turf in any city will belong to crews whose members actually walk. It’s the only thing that counts.
