Here’s the simple bit

Walk a loop.
Paint the block.

Open the app. Start walking. Watch your trail appear behind you in real time. Loop back to where you started — and the whole block floods with your colour. Bigger loop? More turf. It’s really that satisfying.

Every step earns you ink. Ink turns into claimed blocks. Walk daily and your ink pile grows. Sit still and it doesn’t. Neither does your territory.

Your signature

Your colour.
Everywhere you’ve been.

Pick your colour on day one. Walk your city and it lights up in that shade — your blocks, your trails, your mark on the map. Everyone can see where you’ve been. You can see where they haven’t.

No leaderboards. No podiums. Just colour on a map — quietly telling the whole city you were here.

The spicy part

Claimed blocks don’t last forever. Leave them alone and they start fading — first a little orange, then gone. Walk back through and they bounce right back. But you have to actually go. The city doesn’t pause for you.

Don’t worry — we’ll nudge you before things get bad. But if you ignore us long enough, someone else will gladly take what you built.
